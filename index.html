    Bio

    I am a Ph.D. student at Embedded Intelligent Systems(EIS) Lab under the supervision of Prof.JeongGil Ko and jointly advised by Prof.Songkuk Kim.
    From August 2024 to February 2025, I was a visiting researcher at Singapore Management University (SMU) under the supervision of Prof. Rajesh Krishna Balan, and I continue to work closely with the SMU team.
    My primary research interests lie in machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL) applied to mobile and embedded systems, with a particular focus on federated learning.
    Recently, I've been delving into intelligent clinical and healthcare systems, as well as exploring security issues within deep learning models and systems.

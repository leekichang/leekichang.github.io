    Bio

    My name is Kichang Lee. I am an integrated M.S./Ph.D. student at Embedded Intelligent Systems(EIS) Lab under the supervision of Prof.JeongGil Ko.
    My primary research interests lie in machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL) applied to mobile and embedded systems, with a particular focus on federated learning.
    Recently, I've been delving into intelligent clinical and healthcare systems, as well as exploring security issues within deep learning models and systems.
    I received my B.S. degree from the school of integrated technology at Yonsei University.
